1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum)

Text (and images of text) have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1, except if the text is pure decoration. Larger scale text (at least 18 point or 14 point bold) or images of text can have a contrast ratio of 3:1

1.4.6 Contrast (Enhanced)

Text (and images of text) have a contrast ratio of at least 7:1, except if the text is pure decoration. Larger scale text (at least 18 point or 14 point bold) or images of text can have a contrast ratio of 4.5:1

Pass AAA
